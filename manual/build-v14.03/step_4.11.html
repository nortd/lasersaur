Concerning the entire subsystem:

Tips

  • Mounting the door to the frame can be simplified by first mounting just the rear door extrusion and then attaching the rest of the door to this extrusion.
  • The gas spring attachment point on the frame (distance from door pivot) is determining the resistence. Latter has to be adjusted with the weight of the door panels in mind.
  • Door sensors will have to be mounted in colse proximity to the door magnets without beeing hit by the closing door. Exact height adjustment is best done later.

Checklist

  • Door closes without pushing the gas spings all the way in?
  • Gas springs move simultaneously?
  • Symmetric gap between left and right frame?
  • Door's rear slit cover is positioned to minimize any opening in the rear left/right corners?
